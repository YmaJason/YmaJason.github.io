Johnny Depp

About Johnny Depp

- John Christopher Depp II, otherwise known as Johnny Depp was born on June 9th, 1963.
- He grew up in Kentucky, United States with his parents and 3 siblings.
- At the age of 12, Johnny joined various bands to play rock music.
- When Johnny turned 16, he decided to drop out of school to pursue his dreams of becoming a rock musician;
two weeks later, he tried to come back to school but his principal told him to follow his dreams.
- In his early 20s, Johnny struggled as a musician, and decided to pursue acting, making his film debut in the movie "A Nightmare on Elm Street" in 1984.
- Johnny continued to pursue acting and rose to fame during his teenage role in the TV Series "21 Jump Street" (1987-1990).

Achievements

Johnny Depp is an incredible actor that has achieved many significant acting awards in his life time. He has won over 80 awards and has been nominated over 150 times.
Despite being such an icon in the movie industry for such an extensive time, Johnny has never won an Oscar Award for Best Actor and has been nominated three times .
He also has not won an Oscar Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role and has been nominated twice.
Johnny has won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor - Motion Picture Musical or Comedy and has been nominated 10 times.
